Our Content

Content/Topic: Effects of Watching Digital Series across the Internet and Television on junior high school students. We chose “effects of watching digital series across the internet and television to junior high school students” as our topic because it isn't new for all of us to hear that watching digital media has become an incredibly profound trend in our current generation. We are certain that plenty lot of us have the habit of watching series to temporarily escape from our reality and reduce the stress that we often feel due to our academic responsibilities. Because of this, we aim to furthermore understand the effects it brings to junior high school students as we are also one ourselves, we want to exercise our platform to reach the students who lack academic motivation and instead use digital media to cope with the strains of academics.

Streaming Type: Podcast. We chose podcast as our streaming type as it can help build a relationship with our audience. Our voice modulation can help emphasize important views, and will also help them become familiar with our goals and ideas. Furthermore, it is a great medium to cover the key topics of our segment and to show the depth of our topic. And lastly, podcasting is an accessible way to reach our desired audience.

Streaming platform: YouTube. We chose YouTube as our streaming platform because not only is it a free application that can be easily accessible for anyone and everyone no matter what type of device you are using, but it is also one of the largest and most popular video distribution platforms on the Internet. It is also widely known as a platform for learners. With this, not only are we using a platform that can benefit students, but we also have the opportunity of bringing our topic to different kinds of people that are in need of this type of information.

Streaming tool: OBS (Open Broadcaster Software) We chose OBS as our streaming tool as it is a free and open-source solution for offline video recording and live streaming that is Mac and Windows-compliant. It is also said to be the best overall free software in terms of flexibility and power. It has a variety of studio features for instance; multi-screen recording, on-demand recording, screen capture, video capture, screen recording, collaboration tools, audio capture, and file management that are incredibly helpful for our stream.

Promotional Plan

Main Objective: Our goal is to have at least 25-50 viewers on the day of the stream, and our target audience will be junior high school students who are interested in watching digital series.

Marketing Channels: Our stream will be promoted in three ways: content marketing, social media marketing, and email marketing. We will distribute the invitation posters and then publish them on various social media platforms for content marketing or social media marketing. Then, to advertise it more to our target audience, we will utilize email marketing by just mass emailing our schoolmates.
